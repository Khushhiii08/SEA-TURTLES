About the Aquarium

The Navi Mumbai Aquarium is designed to educate visitors about marine biodiversity and conservation. It features multiple zones that replicate natural habitats such as coral reefs, lagoons, and deep-sea environments.

Visitors can learn about aquatic life, environmental sustainability, and the importance of preserving marine ecosystems.
